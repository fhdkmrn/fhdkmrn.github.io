Cavs in Chaos: What's with the Cavaliers Mid-Season Decline?

Jan 26, 2018

The Cleveland Cavaliers currently sit at third place in the Eastern Conference of the NBA, sporting a 27-19 record. They barely sit above the Heat in 4th place, while they are well away from reaching the Raptors and the Celtics in second and first respectively. Much of their disastrous situation is due to their recent form, where they have lost 9 of their last 13 games. Moreover, in the a recent game, the Cavaliers let up 145 against the Thunder, the most points the most regulation points allowed in the teams history. The fingers are being pointed all over the place, from Isaiah Thomas' long-awaited return from a long injury to Tye Lue's coaching style and lineup decisions.
