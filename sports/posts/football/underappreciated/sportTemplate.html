Underappreciated Football Stars in Europe's Top Leagues

Jan 3, 2018

Today, more than ever, superstars such as Messi, Ronaldo, and Neymar dominate the headlines and debates in world football. In fact, the superstar of a big team is more often than not toted as the most influential and important player for the team. For Chelsea, it is Hazard, while for Liverpool, it is currently Salah. This begs the question: Are these superstars really the most important players for these top teams, or are there other players whose work on the pitch are getting overlooked? Join me as I look at some of the top teams where, arguably, the superstar is not the most integral player to the team.
