Fahad Kamran

Research Scientist at Apple.

I am a Research Scientist at Apple on the Health AI team. Before that, I completed my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Michigan, working in the MLD3 lab under Jenna Wiens. Broadly, my research interests are in the intersection of machine learning and healthcare, with focus in causal inference, time-series analysis, large language models, and working with large-scale wearable and electronic health record (EHR) data.

I received my Bachelors degree from the University of California, Berkeley in Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science. Outside of research, I enjoy going to the gym, playing and watching sports, and watching movies and television.
